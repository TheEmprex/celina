Celina

by Maxime

Celina

most beautiful woman on earth

sweet without trying

charming without knowing it

pure heart

rare kind

soft in the ways that matter

strong in the ways that last

it takes a thousand ways

to reach you

and even then

you decide

your silence is stubborn

your voice is trouble

either way

you don't go unnoticed

your smile

that's what beauty is

not the idea of it

not the chase

your smile

your eyes

sharp as polished steel

wild

proud

awake

I swear to God

you are beautiful

the most beautiful girl

I've ever seen

more beautiful

than the details people cling to

your smile

the best thing

my eyes have ever known

I can't remember

how life was

before I met you

rosy cheeks

heavy eyes

like you're half dream

half reality

when the world gets loud

your love

sounds like peace

I believe in the invisible string

because of you

like we were connected

before timing made sense

never pulling

just always there

your laugh

undoes me

every time

even if I couldn't see you

I'd still know

you're beautiful

I'd feel it

in the calm

I want to hold you close

shield you

from anything meant to hurt you

even your scent

lingers

soft

familiar

and if it ever comes down to it

your happiness

your safety

your peace

I'd do it for you

Forever yours, Maxime
